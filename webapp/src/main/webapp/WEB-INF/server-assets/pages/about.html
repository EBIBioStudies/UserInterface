About BioStudies

What is BioStudies?

The goal of the BioStudies database is to provide light-weight means for capturing data from biological studies, especially for the cases where due to the pace of high-throughput technology development the generated data does not fit into any of the specialized EBI databases, or where established technologies are used in combination. The database will be able to accept a wide range of types of studies described via a simple format, and will not impose minimum requirements outside the respective community agreements. It will also enable manuscript authors to submit supplementary information and link to it from the publication.
